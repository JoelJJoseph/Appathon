Level - 1 Audiobooks

The Red Raincoat

This story is Manu who has a new raincoat. He can't wait to wear it, but the rain makes him wait... and wait... and wait. It is written by Kiran Kasturi

My Fish, no my fish!!

It is a funny little story about three friends and three clever fish! It is written by Suraj J Menon.

The Race

This story is about four children. Four toys. They decide to a have a race! Who will win? It is wriiten by Kanchan Bannerjee.

I want that one!

This book is about Anil. "Not that one," says Anil's mother when he asks for something. "Not that one!" say all the shopkeepers in the market too. That makes the little boy very angry indeed! It is written by Mala Kumar.

Level - 2 Audiobooks

Ammachi's Amazing Machines

This story is about Sooraj and his grandma LOVE inventing! Join them on their latest adventure: using simple machines to make coconut barfi! it is written by Rajiv Eipe.

Annual Haircut Day

This story is about Sringeri Srinivas, the lovable farmer with very long hair, wants it cut on Annual Haircut Day. But, everyone is busy. Read this story to find out who helps him get a haircut.It is written by Rohini Nilekani.

Singing in the Rain

This story is about Sukhiya Kaka was a famous singer. He met Dinoo who turned out to be his biggest fan. Together they made quite a pair! It is written by Manisha Chaudhry and Mala Kumar.

Rabbit Becomes Cheif.

This story is about raw carrots for breakfast. Raw carrots for lunch. Raw carrots for dinner. Rabbit is tired of eating raw carrots and wants to learn how to cook them into a tasty curry. It is written by Chammi Iresha.

Level - 3 Audiobooks

The girl who could not stop laughing.

This story is about T. Sundari. Every day T. Sundari gets into trouble because most things make her laugh. She just cannot keep her mouth sealed. T. Sundari wants to know if something is wrong with her. Because if there isn't, she can laugh all the time! It is written by Meera Ganapathi.

A book for Pichu

This story is about Puchku. Who has run out of books to read. Then she discovers more books in the top shelf of the library bookcase. But Puchku is small, and the bookcase tall. How will she ever get to her beloved books? it is written by Deepanjana Pal.

A butterfly Smile.

This book is about Kavya. Kavya’s family has just moved from her village to Bengaluru and she is the newest girl in her class. Go along with Kavya as she visits a butterfly park where she discovers a special connection with butterflies and also makes a new friend. It is written by Mathangi Subramanian.

Jadav and the tree palace.

This story is about Jadav. He has the best job in the world: he makes forests! How does he do it? Read this book to find out! It is written by Vinayak Varma.
